The Platform

Every tool you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built from the ground up for carrier operations — not adapted from generic freight software. Every feature reflects how dispatch floors actually work.

01🗺️
Route Optimization
Multi-constraint VRP solving via OR-Tools and HERE Truck Routing. HOS windows, bridge weight limits, hazmat roads, offload rates, and terminal availability. Maximum deliveries, minimum miles.
02📡
Live Fleet Tracking
Real-time GPS via Samsara and Motive API connectors. Driver status, HOS remaining, exception alerts, and geofence events — all on one live map updated continuously.
03⛽
Tank Monitoring
Live customer site levels via radar and pressure probe sensors. Runout predictions and automated delivery suggestions. Managed as a service — one contract, one contact.
04📋
Order Management
Create, prioritize, and track deliveries from order through proof of delivery. Auto-generated order and PO numbers. Add to route or dispatch direct with a single click.
05🔧
Fleet Health
Maintenance schedules, fault codes, driver status, and inspection records. Predictive alerts designed to surface issues before they disrupt a delivery run.
06💰
Billing & Invoicing
Auto-generated invoices on delivery completion. Stripe-powered payment links. Live accounts receivable dashboard. Per-customer pricing rules and fuel surcharge calculators built in.
07📆
Scheduler
Build recurring delivery schedules that auto-create orders for a rolling four weeks. Set frequency, product, carrier, and delivery type — orders populate automatically.
08📄
Contracts
Manage sell contracts for customers, buy product contracts for suppliers, and buy freight contracts for carriers — all linked to orders and invoicing automatically.
09◇
Pricing & Quoting
Per-customer pricing rules, volume tiers, and fuel surcharge calculators. Build and send formal quotes as PDFs directly from the platform.
How It All Connects

One platform.
Everything in sync.

Every module in POLLY shares the same data. An order created in Orders populates in Route Optimization. A customer added in Customers flows through to Scheduler and Billing. Changes in one place reflect everywhere.

🗺
Orders → Routes
Orders created with "Add to Route Builder" automatically appear as stops in Route Optimization, mapped to the customer's coordinates. Route status syncs back to the order.
⛽
Tanks → Deliveries
Live tank levels from customer sites flow directly into inventory intelligence and route planning. POLLY surfaces runout risks before a customer has to call.
📆
Schedules → Orders
Recurring schedules in the Scheduler automatically generate orders on the defined cadence, pre-populated with customer, product, carrier, and delivery type.
📄
Contracts → Pricing
Sell contracts set at the customer level flow through to invoicing automatically. Buy contracts for carriers and suppliers inform margin calculations without manual entry.
💰
Delivery → Invoice
When a delivery is confirmed, POLLY generates and sends the invoice automatically using the customer's pricing rules, then tracks payment through the AR dashboard.
📡
Fleet → Dispatch
Live HOS data from ELD connectors flows into route optimization. POLLY only assigns stops within a driver's available hours, preventing violations before they happen.
