The Platform

Every tool you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built from the ground up for carrier operations — not adapted from generic freight software. Every feature reflects how dispatch floors actually work.

01🗺️
Route Optimization
Multi-constraint Routing. HOS windows, bridge weight, hazmat roads, offload rates, terminal availability. Maximum deliveries, minimum miles.
02📡
Live Fleet Tracking
Real-time GPS monitoring. Exception flags, driver status, and geofence alerts — all on one live map updated every 30 seconds.
03⛽
Tank Monitoring
Live customer site levels via radar and pressure probe sensors. Runout predictions and automated delivery suggestions. Managed as a service — one contract, one contact.
04📋
Order Management
Create, prioritize, and track deliveries from order through proof of delivery. Emergency orders surface instantly. Add to route or dispatch direct — one click.
05🔧
Fleet Health
Maintenance schedules, fault codes, driver status, and inspection records. Predictive alerts before a breakdown disrupts a delivery run.
06💰
Billing & Invoicing
Auto-generated invoices on delivery completion. Stripe payment links. Live AR dashboard. Per-customer pricing rules, volume tiers, and surcharge calculators.
